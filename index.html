Health Issues

Health is a top priority for Kenyan youth, encompassing various critical challenges including:

  • Sexually Transmitted Diseases (STDs): The prevalence of STDs remains a significant concern.
  • HIV/AIDS: The fight against HIV/AIDS continues, with efforts to provide education and healthcare.
  • Drug Abuse: Substance abuse is increasing among the youth, which poses numerous health risks.

Mental Health

Mental health issues are increasingly common among Kenyan youth. Key concerns include:

  • Depression: Rising levels of depression are noted among the youth population.
  • Anxiety: Anxiety disorders are growing, impacting daily lives and productivity.
  • Easting Disorders: Issues related to body image and eating habits have become prevalent.
  • Psychosis: Mental health awareness is crucial in addressing severe disorders like psychosis.

Reproductive Health

Reproductive health issues pose significant challenges:

  • Teenage Pregnancies: The rate of teenage pregnancies remains alarmingly high.
  • Unsafe Abortions: A lack of safe reproductive options leads to dangerous practices.
